Welcome to BYONewtown, website of Newtown Environmental Action Team (NEAT).

NEAT is a nonpartisan grassroots group of Newtown, CT citizens who work together on smart solutions to environmental challenges.

Take a look around the site to see what we're working on. If you see any projects you'd like to be a part of, let us know. We'd love to have you on our team!
